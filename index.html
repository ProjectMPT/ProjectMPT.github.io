Project MPT
Helping Humans Help Humans
Project MPT is new tool that allows you to help people in your local community. It's a decentralized process that uses small self organized groups to make it convenient and rewarding to get involved.
The aid process has three steps.
1A seeker will meet with a person in need and establish a need request in the Project MPT mobile app.
2A provider is able to see all the needs in the local area and commit to provide a requested item.
3A transporter will commit to transport the item, pick it up, and deliver it to the person in need.
Making a difference in someone's life is as simple as downloading the mobile app and requesting a blanket for a person in need, buying it, or simply bringing the blanket to them.
